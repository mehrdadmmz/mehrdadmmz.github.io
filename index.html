    Mehrdad is a computer-science undergrad at Simon Fraser University in Beautiful British Columbia . Most days he’s either training PyTorch models to untangle noisy real-world data or tinkering with Java, building full-stack apps with Spring Boot, JPA, and a slim React front end. He still enjoys the low-level thrill of C++ and likes spinning up cloud-friendly microservices in Go.

    Away from the keyboard you’ll catch him hiking coastal trails, playing soccer, lifting at the gym, knocking out Rubik’s cubes, or reading (right now it’s The Mountain Is You). If a project mixes algorithms with real-world impact, chances are he’s already sketching ideas for it on the nearest whiteboard.
